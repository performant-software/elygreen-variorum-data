On Sundays my step father would be home with my mother. That was why I could go away for a while: I would come in and tell her how wonderful things were. Very soon my mother became so sick that I could not wait on her: Father had to get some. one a woman to care for. I had my little brother then to look after: Father built another room on to the one we had been living in where we cooked slept and bathed in: Father Gerry come Pastor of St Agustus came every morning. He and Dr Hall. Father would pray: And before he would he would leave. he would talk to me telling to stay close to her. many of the white family would drive up in carrages bringing somthing for her. They were so genorous in every way. So one morning Farther ask me would I like to go home with him. I went he only lived a block from us. This was the first time I had gone to any one s home. We went in. He had three children Elexandra. Annie Edward. his wife whom we called mother. She often taught neighborn children like a kindergarten: So almost every day except Sunday. I would go. with father home to play with the children at recess. Sometimes stayed all night because I was in the way. at home: and Mother Gerry was just about the sweetest lady I ever knew: She was so. kind. I did not attend Class althoug She explain any thing I ask her and treated me like other children. This al so made me very happey. Just here is the first time I had to learn something about religions: I hadnt ever seen may mother go to church nor my step farther. So one morning before Farther Gerry came Mother ask me not to go over to the Gerry home: That Elder Trouble was coming. And I might be need. So when He arrived with him was four women: They came. I took my little out side to play. They sang and prayed for many hours.
